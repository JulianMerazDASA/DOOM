Imp

One of the first enemies. Can attack when near and can throw fireballs. It also runs around and can climb walls.

Cacodemon

My favorite demon because of it's design. It's weakness is it's eye and throwing a grenade in it's mouth. It flies around, bites you, and shoots fireballs.

Mancubus

Has arm cannons to shoot you and can leave an effect on the ground to block it off. It's weaknesses are it's arms.

Makyr Drone

Shoots beams at you and dodges attacks. It's weakness is it's head, you have to snipe it but it drops a lot of ammo.

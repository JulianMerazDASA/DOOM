DOOM Eternal Weapons and Demons

Weapons

  • Combat Shotgun
  • Plasma Rifle
  • Ballista
  • Chaingun

Combat Shotgun

First weapon you get, uses shells, weakest in the game. Gets grenades or revolving barrel mods

Plasma Rifle

Comes from the first DOOM game. Use energy ammmo. You can unlock Microwave Beam and Heat Blast mods. Can be used to destroy energy shields.

Ballista

Uses the same ammo as the Plasma Rifle. Shoots a powerful beam and takes time to recharge. Does extra damage to flying demons like the Cacodemon.

Chaingun

Uses the same ammo as the Heavy Cannon. Rapidly shoots bullets at enemy. A shield mod to protext you and Mobile Turret to shoot faster but can overheat. Strong against big, slow demons.

Demons

  • Imp
  • Cacodemon
  • Mancubus
  • Makyr Drone

Imp

One of the first enemies. Can attack when near and can throw fireballs. It also runs around and can climb walls.

Cacodemon

My favorite demon because of its design. Its weakness is its eye and throwing a grenade in its mouth. It flies around, bites you, and shoots fireballs.

Mancubus

Has arm cannons to shoot you and can leave an effect on the ground to block it off. Its weaknesses are its arms.

Makyr Drone

Shoots beams at you and dodges attacks. Its weakness is its head, you have to snipe it but it drops a lot of ammo.
